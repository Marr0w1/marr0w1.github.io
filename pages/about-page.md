gumbo (noun)
uk  /ˈɡʌm.bəʊ/ us  /ˈɡʌm.boʊ/
"a thick soup from the southern US, made with okra (= a small green vegetable) and meat or fish"

Aiming to be as diverse and satisfying as a big stew, we play a wide mix of Americana classics, from old-time and country classics through to western swing, blues and bluegrass. 
A hearty mix of well known staples, seasoned with a few lesser known flavours.
